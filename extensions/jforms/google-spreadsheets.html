Description

Take data-collection in Joomla! to the next level. Create custom forms and populate Google Spreadsheets with the entered data.

Keep it private as a data storage for surveys or a back-up of your website's contact requests or share the spreadsheet with other users to for example track guestlist signups for events or evaluate survey data.

Using the Google Spreadsheets API the process is instant so your Google Spreadsheet data updates in real-time.
